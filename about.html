Resume

Karen Ludington

Summary

Figure skating coach with over 20 years experience. Provide group and private lessons as well as team coaching support in figures, moves, freestyle, choreography and movement, with an emphasis on the essential figure skating vocabulary and movement principles that support connected, expressive, safe figure skating.

Coaching & Choreography

  • Teach group and private lessons in field moves, figures, freestyle, off-ice movement and choreography.
  • 2011 Eastern Sectional Coach. 2007 Jr. National coach.
  • Choreographer, Potomac Ice Theatre ensemble teams, 2000-01 season.

Program Direction & Support

  • Board Member, Pro Skating Historical Foundation, 2008-June 2013.
  • Judge, Audrey Weisiger’s Young Artist’s Showcase, a choreography competition for skaters, seasons 2-5 (2011-14).
  • Board Member, Professional Figure Skaters Cooperative (now called ProSkaters), 2007-2008.
  • Director, Skate Frederick summer ice show, 2004.
  • Skating Director, Kentlands Outdoor Ice Rink. Developed, directed recreational lesson program in Gaithersburg, MD, 2002-03.
  • Program Manager, Ice Theatre of New York's Learn to Ice Dance program. Prepared grants, managed program budget, directed teaching staff for educational dance and skating program for New York City public school students, 1998.

Performance Experience

  • Chorus Skater/Performance Director, Holiday Spectacular on Ice, AEG Ice, Fall 2001.
  • Modern Dancer, ArtFarm Performance Café, Kitty Clark & Cam Millar, Fall 2000.
  • Ensemble Skater, Holiday Spectacular on Ice, Jon Sheridan & Ken Shelley, Fall 1997.
  • Ensemble Skater, Ice Theatre of New York (various), Moira North, 1996-1997.

Education & Professional Memberships

  • Member: USFS, WFSC, PSA, ISI. CER Cat A coach 2010-15. PSA Rated (CM, CFS) and ranked (Level III ranking).
  • USFSA Gold Test Medalist in Figures, Freestyle, Field Moves; Pre-Gold Dance Test. Coached by: Joan and Robert Ogilvie (through Intermediate); Gerard Renaud (Novice-Sr.). Also coached by Audrey Weisiger, Ken Class, DIane Agle (Choreo). Ice Dance: Pat Summers, Robbie Kaine. Also coached by Max Bolotin, Igor Lukanin.
  • Certified Laban Movement Analyst (CMA). Graduate-level certificate, 1993-95, Laban/Bartenieff Institute of Movement Studies, NYC. (Program held at University of Maryland College Park).
  • Dance: Extensive training in ballet and modern dance, including at the Erick Hawkins School for Dance, NYC, 1996-99. Currently studying GYROTONIC® Expansion System & GYROKINESIS® exercise. Level 1 Apprentice Teacher, GYROKINESIS® exercise.
  • Bryn Mawr College, Bryn Mawr, PA. BA in Computer Science.
